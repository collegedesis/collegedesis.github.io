After 3 years of national scale projects that brought together dance teams, a cappella groups, Greek life and others, it's time to retire the CollegeDesis brand name.

Dear everyone,

I started CollegeDesis 3 years ago with a Twitter account and it quickly became a nationally recognized brand. My goal was to open up channels of communication among the hundreds of South Asian student organizations in America and to encourage them to collaborate on projects larger than themselves.

Although we did some remarkable things, I have decided to move on to other things.

If you worked with CollegeDesis in the last three years, you worked with me (even if you didn't know it) and I would love to stay in touch. I live in the San Francisco Bay Area, so please get in touch!

Mehul Kar August 15, 2014
